About

Artist, Craftsman & Student

I am a 20-year-old student studying at American University, interested in fine arts and business. I have spent the last few years developing my skills in art handling, framing, and hanging at and for various locations, including Mana Contemporary and The Bryne Art Gallery, as well as through volunteer and personal work.

Being from NYC, my whole life I have been surrounded by music and art, leading me to a fixated interest in these subjects. I have spent my time honing my artistic talent, specifically in woodworking and metalwork, as those are favorites among mine to work with.

What inspires me to create is the profound sense of loneliness the world would have without art. I find that all forms of art help us connect person to person in a way that not many other things can. Evoking emotion and connection is among the highest abilities for a person to make — and is what I strive for.
